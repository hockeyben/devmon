- Small/Common: 8-10 lines tall
- Max 20 visual columns wide
- Rich markup for coloring (D-05): use [bold red], [dim green], etc.

- Dense: @, #, %
- Medium: *, +, =, ~
- Light/outline: /, \, |, -, _
